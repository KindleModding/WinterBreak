1. Copy everything in the `MountSus` tar.gz file to your Kindle root
2. Open mountsus app in mesquito
3. Wait 1-2 mins for it to initialise
4. Click JAILBREAK
5. Wait 1-2 mins for it to compute stuff
6. Click the jailbreak button on the popup
7. Once the popup tells you do, close it with the `X`
8. Scroll down and click `VERIFY JAILBREAK`
9. Reboot if it tells you to
