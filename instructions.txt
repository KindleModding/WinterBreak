1. install Mesquito
2. copy everything in the `MountSus` folder to your Kindle root
3. open mountsus app in mesquito
4. wait 1-2 mins for it to initialise
5. click JAILBREAK
6. wait 1-2 mins for it to compute stuff
7. click the jailbreak button on the popup
8. once the popup tells you do, close it with the `X`
9. scroll down and click `VERIFY JAILBREAK`
10. reboot if it tells you to
